Chicken Biryani

Discription

Biryani (/bɜːrˈjɑːni/) is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, and fish), or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  • 600 gm chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala powder.
